Boxing concepts that you will learn

  • All
  • Attack
  • Defense
  • Movements
Straight punches4 weeks
The most well-known type of punches in boxing and also the fastest and most commonly used
Guard.2 weeks
The correct stance in guard is undoubtedly the most valuable thing a beginner boxer can learn. In fact, all the movements and punches in boxing start from the guard, so a correct guard is absolutely necessary.
Hooks6 weeks
Known as the most powerful punches in boxing, hooks are responsible for the most spectacular knockouts in history!
Blocks6 weeks
Blocks represent the most fundamental part of defense in boxing. There are several types of blocks, but the most important ones are named after the main punches: straight blocks, hook blocks, and uppercut blocks.
Boxing steps3 weeks
If you have ever watched a boxing match, you have noticed that this is how boxers move. By adding boxing steps, it ensures a position that is both secure and natural to move around the ring.
Uppercuts.6 weeks
Also, the most devastating punches in boxing. A properly executed uppercut can not only knock out the opponent but can even take their breath away for several minutes!
Dodges6 weeks
Just like blocks, dodges are also based on the three types of punches. They are highly agile and unpredictable movements that create opportunities for counterattacks.
Level changes4 weeks
Indispensable to a good boxer, level changes have the potential to turn the tide of a match. A boxer who executes a well-timed level change while in the corner can expose their opponent and even reverse their position.
Balance1 săptămână
Hand in hand with the boxing steps is the balance. When boxers are not moving, they never remain completely still. By maintaining balance, reflexes and speed are significantly enhanced, which is essential for gaining an advantage.

Boxing bags with different heights and resistances

Purchased for superior quality, our boxing bags offer perfect durability for the optimal progression of the boxer. Made of resilient material and available in different heights and resistances, they are perfect for any age.

Various gloves, suitable for you

We have the two fundamental types of gloves. Perhaps you didn't know, but it's important to wear different gloves depending on the exercise. Bag gloves are thinner, so they are not recommended for use in the ring. Sparring gloves are larger and thicker, providing protection for both you and your opponent.

Adjustable headgear for all sizes

The headgear is part of the latest models, featuring a compact closure. The Spartan-like design provides protection for the back, sides, and front of the head.

Mobile hand guards and wall pads

In addition to boxing bags, we have hand guards and wall pads. Hand guards are worn by the trainer or workout partner and are used for dynamically practicing punches through movement. Wall pads, on the other hand, are fixed to the wall. Unlike the bag, which moves upon impact, wall pads remain stationary.

Various equipment

Boxing is not just about fighting. In order to master boxing techniques and tactics, maintaining a proper physical condition is crucial. We offer various equipment for both cardio and strength training. Some of these include jump ropes, dumbbells, agility cones, speed balls, medicine balls, resistance bands, specific ropes, and more.

Events/competitions

Events/competitions you can participate in

Romanian Boxing Cup.



The Romanian Boxing Cup is an annual event organized by the Romanian Boxing Federation, bringing together boxers from across the country to compete in a high-class boxing tournament. This competition is one of the most important boxing events in Romania, where athletes can showcase their skills and talent in front of an enthusiastic audience. The Romanian Boxing Cup caters to both the youth and senior categories, providing opportunities for boxers to compete in different weight and age divisions. The competition takes place throughout the year, with local and regional qualifying stages followed by the national finals. Winners of the competition are awarded with medals, diplomas, and other prizes, and this event serves as an opportunity to make a name for oneself and stand out in the Romanian boxing community.

Balkan Boxing Championships



This international competition brings together boxers from various Balkan countries, including Romania, Bulgaria, Turkey, Greece, and others. It serves as a competitive environment aimed at promoting the sport. The Balkan Boxing Championships are divided into age categories, including juniors, youth, and seniors. The competition provides boxers with an opportunity to compete at an international level and showcase their fighting skills. Additionally, winners can receive cash prizes, trophies, and other rewards. The competition takes place in different countries in the Balkan region and attracts athletes from all over the world. It is also an important event for coaches, boxing officials, and sports fans who come to support the boxers and share their knowledge about the sport.

European Boxing Championship



The European Boxing Championship is one of the most prestigious international male amateur boxing competitions in Europe, organized by the European Boxing Confederation (EUBC). This sporting event attracts the best amateur boxers from across Europe, competing for the title of European champion in various weight categories. The competition consists of a series of elimination rounds, followed by finals, where athletes must showcase high-level technical and tactical skills to secure victory. Participants are assessed by competent referees who evaluate the scoring of each round based on the number of accurate and effective punches landed. The European Boxing Championship provides an excellent platform for young boxers to showcase their abilities and test their limits against world-class athletes. Furthermore, this sporting event serves as an opportunity to promote the sport and encourage an active and healthy lifestyle.
